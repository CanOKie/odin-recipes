Baked Potato

Classic Baked Potato

This classic baked potato is great for beginners. It is easy to do, takes little prep, and requires NOprevious cooking experience. Best of all, you can customize it to your liking.

So, go ahead and pick your favorite spud and get ready for greatness!

Ingredients

  • One Large Potato
  • Generous Amounts of Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • Shreaded Cheese of your Choice
  • 1 Strip of Cooked Bacon
  • Handful of Diced Onion
  • Add Diced Green and Red Peppers

Steps

  1. Pre-heat Your Oven to 400F
  2. While the Oven heats up, clean Your Potato. Remove any blemishes and make sure to poke several time with a fork
  3. When the Oven is ready, place the potato directly on the oven rack
  4. Set Your Timer for One Hour
  5. While You Wait, get your butter and shreaded cheese ready. Cut up your onions, Red and Green Peppers. Make sure the bacon is cooked and torn into bite size pieces
  6. OK, the hour is up, feel the potato to ensure its soft. BE CAREFUL THE POTATO IS HOT!If not put it back in for 5 mins and check again.
  7. Once the Potato is cooked, remove from the oven.
  8. Cut open the Potato immediately and insert butter, salt, and pepper.
  9. Add the Cheese
  10. Add the onion and peppers
  11. Add the bacon on top.
  12. Voila!Your Baked Potato is Ready!
